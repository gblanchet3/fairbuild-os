## ICP

- **Company type:** Construction material suppliers and subcontractors
- **Revenue:** $15M – $100M
- **Employees:** 50 – 200
- **Structure:** No in-house counsel
- **Pain:** High exposure to one-sided GC agreements; contracts signed under time pressure; no good review process

## Primary Buyer Persona

- **Title:** Owner, President, EVP, VP of Operations
- **Decision authority:** Full — writes the check, no committee
- **Tech skill:** Limited. Lives in email, phone, Excel. Probably not in Slack. Maybe ChatGPT for one-off tasks.
- **Time pressure:** Intense. Running the business, has 20 minutes not a day.
- **Trust cues:** Industry-specific language, referrals from associations (SBCA, LBM dealers), peers who've used it

## Key Trades

1. **Truss plants / SBCA members** — market entry beachhead
2. **LBM dealers** (lumber and building materials)
3. **Framers**
4. **Other subcontractors** (electrical, mechanical, millwork, etc.)
