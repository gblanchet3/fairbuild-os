## Mission

Rewrite the balance of power in subcontract negotiations.

## What We Do

AI-powered risk management for construction subcontractors and suppliers. Upload a subcontract → get risk analysis + redline suggestions in ~3 minutes. Saves 2–8 hours per contract and protects against catastrophic contract losses.

## The Problem

Three-part: **Lack of Time + Lack of Expertise = Contract Risk.**

- Subcontracts are written by the GC's lawyers with one goal: shift risk downstream.
- Senior leaders at subs are trade experts, not lawyers.
- Hiring a lawyer costs hundreds to thousands per contract; too slow, too expensive relative to contract value.
- 83% of 400+ LinkedIn survey respondents said they'd suffered significant financial consequences from lack of effective contract review.

## Why Now

- **Bigger GCs:** Top 50 GCs went from 48% → 60% of ENR Top 400 revenue (2014–2024).
- **Bigger projects:** Multifamily >50 units went from 25% → 55% of projects (2004 vs 2024).
- Bigger GCs + bigger projects = more aggressive risk-shifting in subcontracts.

## Competitive Position

| Alternative | Why we win |
|---|---|
| Hiring a lawyer | 100s–1000s per contract, slow, kills deals |
| Construction law AI (Document Crunch, Provision, SuperLegal, SpeedLegal) | Built for GCs or 4x our price; enterprise, not SMB |
| Legal AI (Tiro, Ivo, Legartis, Spellbook) | Lawyer perspective, not business perspective |
| Generic LLMs | Inconsistent, miss operational details, fail to note omissions |

**Our wedge:** purpose-built for the subcontractor/supplier owner who is not a lawyer, not technical, and has 20 minutes — not a day — to turn around a contract.

## Product

- Upload contract → analyze in ~3 min
- Review highlighted risks, filtered by customer ranking
- Choose issues to negotiate; decline lower-priority items
- Incorporate suggested language into redline, or edit to refine
- Publish redline, return to GC
- **Time savings: 2–8 hours per contract**
