## Core Principles

1. **Builder-to-builder.** Talk like one construction owner to another. Not like a SaaS company talking to a "user."
2. **Plainspoken.** No legalese. No "leverage," "solutions," "stakeholders," "value-add."
3. **Money language.** Lead with dollars lost, projects saved, hours back in the day. Not "clauses" and "provisions."
4. **Real stories beat stats.** Every post, ad, and email anchored in a real war story (anonymized where needed).
5. **Respect their time.** Our buyer has 20 minutes. So does our reader.

## Words We Use

- Risk, exposure, margin, loss
- Redline, negotiate, push back
- Owner, operator, field
- GC, sub, supplier
- Minutes, not hours

## Words We Don't Use

- Leverage, synergy, ecosystem
- Solution (prefer: "tool" or name the specific thing)
- Disruption
- Game-changer, paradigm shift
- Counsel, provision, clause (use: "lawyer," "what the contract says," "the part about…")

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn post | First-person, story-first, one insight per post |
| Instagram ad | Visual-first, short copy, one hook + one CTA |
| Cold email | Short, specific to trade, referencing a real pain |
| Founder video | Direct-to-camera, 60 sec max, one story + one offer |
| Sales demo | Let the product do the talking; set up the "oh wow" moment |
