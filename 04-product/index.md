# Product Overview

## What It Does

Uploads a subcontract (PDF or DOCX), analyzes it in ~3 minutes, returns:

- Highlighted risks, ranked by severity
- Filter by customer ranking (high-priority risks first)
- Suggested language / redline for each risk
- Option to accept, edit, or decline each suggestion
- Published redline returned to the GC

## Time Savings

2 to 8 hours per contract.

## "Oh Wow" Moment

12-page agreement → risk report in 3 minutes. The moment the redline comes back is the moment the prospect becomes a customer.

## Who It's For

Owners/executives at subcontractor and supplier firms ($15M–$100M rev, 50–200 employees) with no in-house counsel and high exposure to one-sided GC agreements.

## Pricing

- $1,000 for 5 contracts
- $3,000 for 30 contracts
- $4,000 for 50 contracts
- Enterprise: custom

Annual prepay. ~88%+ gross margin.

## Under the Hood (for context)

- ~$1.00 processing cost per page
- Average 12 pages per contract = ~$12 processing cost
- 30 contracts at $3,000 price = $2,640 margin if fully utilized

## Product Strategy

**Phase 1:** High-ROI contract review and negotiating tool
**Phase 2:** Indispensable contract intelligence platform — proprietary data from thousands of analyzed contracts becomes negotiated-terms intelligence for users
