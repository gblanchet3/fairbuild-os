## The $180K story

A subcontractor, under the terms of their agreement, had to keep working on a project where the owner had stopped paying the GC. With a proper contract review up front, the exposure could have been cut off after the first unpaid invoice. Instead, the sub worked for months without pay — loss ran to **$180,000**.

**Angle for content:** "The clause that costs $180K." One post. One ad. One cold email hook.
